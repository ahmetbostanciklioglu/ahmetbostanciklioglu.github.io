Privacy Policy — AntiqueAI

Antique & Vintage Identifier · Pocket Appraiser
No accounts · No tracking · No ads

Effective date: May 26, 2026

AntiqueAI ("the App") identifies and values antiques, vintage items, and estate-sale finds using AI. We care about your privacy. This policy explains what data we handle. We do not sell your data. We do not run advertising trackers. We do not require an account.

1. Photos & Camera

To identify an antique, you photograph it or pick an image from your library. The image is sent to our AI provider (see §3) for identification only. We do not store your photos on our servers. Camera and photo-library access are used solely so you can capture/select images for identification.

2. Collection & Local Storage

If you save an identified item to "My Collection", the following is stored locally on your iPhone only:

  • Photo (JPEG in your app sandbox)
  • Era, style, maker, materials, estimated value range, authenticity markers
  • Your notes
  • Free-tier counter (3 IDs / 24 h, tracked locally)

None of this is uploaded to our servers. Deleting the App removes everything.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini AI (Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite Vision) — receives the antique image and returns identification + value-range data. We do not transmit personally identifying information. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase Edge Functions — relays our API credentials to authorized clients. No user data passes through this layer. See Supabase Privacy.
  • Apple StoreKit — handles optional AntiqueAI Pro subscriptions. We never see payment details. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Valuation Disclaimer

Estimated values are AI-generated and indicative only. They are not professional appraisals. For insurance, sale, or estate purposes, consult a certified appraiser.

5. Subscriptions & Purchases

AntiqueAI Pro is an auto-renewable subscription processed by Apple. Manage or cancel in iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

6. Analytics & Diagnostics

No third-party analytics, no advertising SDKs, no tracking pixels. Crash diagnostics use Apple's standard opt-in mechanism.

7. Children's Privacy

AntiqueAI is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

  • Delete the App to remove all local data and saved collection items.
  • Manage your subscription in iOS Settings → Subscriptions.
  • Contact us (see §10) to ask about data we hold.

9. Changes to This Policy

Significant changes will be noted with a revised effective date at the top of this page.
