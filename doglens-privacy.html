Privacy Policy — DogLens

Dog Breed Identifier
No data collected · Works 100% on-device

Effective date: May 20, 2026

DogLens (“the App”) is a dog breed identification app. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information the App does and does not handle. In short: DogLens does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. No Data Collection

DogLens does not require an account and does not collect personal information. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking, or third-party data collection of any kind. We have no servers that receive your data.

2. Photos & Camera

Breed identification runs entirely on your device using an offline machine-learning model. When you take a photo or choose one from your library, the image is analyzed locally and is never uploaded to us or any third party. The App does not store, copy, or transmit your photos. Camera and photo-library access are used solely to let you select an image to identify.

3. Subscriptions & Purchases

DogLens offers an optional auto-renewable subscription (DogLens Pro). All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details, card information, or Apple ID. Purchase and subscription management are handled by Apple under Apple’s Privacy Policy.

4. On-Device Storage

The App stores only basic, non-personal settings on your device (for example, whether you have completed onboarding). This never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the App.

5. Children’s Privacy

DogLens is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Third Parties

The App does not contain advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking technologies. The only external service involved is Apple’s App Store for optional subscriptions.

7. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete. Deleting the App removes all locally stored settings from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
