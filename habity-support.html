Frequently asked questions

I'm not getting notifications.

Open iOS Settings → Notifications → Habity and make sure "Allow Notifications" is on. Then open Habity → Reminders and confirm a time is set for each habit you want to be reminded about.

How do streaks work?

Your streak counts consecutive scheduled days you've completed a habit. A habit scheduled only on weekdays won't break its streak on a Sunday. Today's habit doesn't break your streak until midnight.

Can I edit or delete a habit?

Tap a habit on the Home screen to open Habit Detail, then tap Edit (top-right). From there you can rename, change days, edit the reminder, or delete the habit and its history.

I tapped a past day on the calendar but it didn't mark.

Tapping a day in the calendar selects it (you'll see a white ring). Then tap the floating Mark button at the bottom of Habit Detail to toggle that selected day's completion.

Is my data backed up?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Habity does not run accounts or cloud sync. Deleting the App permanently removes all stored data.

Is the app free?

Yes. Habity is free with no ads and no third-party tracking. See our Privacy Policy.

I want to change my profile photo.

Open the Profile tab and tap the avatar circle. iOS will ask you to grant access to a photo (you can grant a single photo at a time). The chosen photo is stored locally and never uploaded.

I changed my mind about the photo. How do I remove it?

Tap the avatar and pick a different photo, or delete the App to fully reset all stored data.
